About Us

Seafront SEPO Grill & Restaurant

A leading culinary establishment located in Tagbilaran City, Bohol.

A proud member of Hotel & Restaurant Association of the Philippines (HRAP) and operating under the esteemed Seafront SEPO brand, we are committed to delivering exceptional dining experiences to our patrons.

With two prime locations inside the Galleria and Alturas malls, we cater to a diverse range of customers, offering a variety of delicious grilled dishes and restaurant specialties.
Conveniently located at V.P Inting Avenue, Mansasa Seaside, Tagbilaran City, our main office is just a short distance away from major business hubs, allowing us to efficiently serve our clients and partners.

Seafront SEPO Grill & Restaurant

Grill & Restaurant

  • Indulge in a delightful dining experience at Seafront Sepo Grill and Restaurant. Our menu features a wide variety of grilled dishes and restaurant specialties, prepared with the freshest ingredients and crafted to perfection by our skilled chefs.

    • Ball Dancing

  • Join us every Friday night for an elegant and entertaining evening of ball dancing. Whether you're an experienced dancer or just starting out, our venue offers the perfect atmosphere for a night of rhythm and grace. Plus, we provide professional dance instructors to guide you.

    • Comedy Bar

  • Add a hilarious twist to your evenings at our Comedy Bar, where laughter is guaranteed. Enjoy a night of entertainment with performances from talented comedians, making it the perfect way to unwind and have fun with friends and family. Plus, unleash your inner rockstar with our Karaoke session for added amusement.

    • Catering Service

  • Let us bring the flavors of Seafront Sepo Grill and Restaurant to your event. Our catering service offers a diverse menu, customizable to suit your preferences and dietary needs. We provide everything from appetizers to desserts, ensuring your event is a culinary success.
